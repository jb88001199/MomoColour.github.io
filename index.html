Our stories are read in many different languages and translations are provided in English and sometimes Mandarin. We seek to provide the best quality recordings, and our article layouts are made to maximize ease of reading and understanding.

Our club is a group of students from an internationally diverse community that speaks many languages.

Even something as small as listening to stories can prepare children for the internationally oriented society we have today. Our stories are reviewed, peer-reviewed, then edited by editors with lots of experience in both English and Mandarin. The stories we select are carefully selected based on each language, for example, if a story was told in German, the story would be a German children story. This gives children not only an internationally diverse experience in language, but also in the culture that is needed to thrive in modern society.

We have told stories in German, French, English, Cantonese, Mandarin, Korean, Malay and Nepali.
